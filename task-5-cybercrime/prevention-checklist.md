# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never approve unknown UPI payment requests
- Do not share OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone
- Always verify links before clicking
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly check bank statements for suspicious activity
- Install apps only from trusted sources
- For UPI safety: Never scan unknown QR codes
- For UPI safety: Do not accept "collect requests" from strangers

## Reporting
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
